ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Aken, a front-end developer based in Toronto. I am excited about creating accessible, responsive and engaging websites with clean and dry code.

I am a graduate of HackerYou’s Front-End Immersive Bootcamp. I am constantly striving to improve my skill set, and I jump at any opportunity to learn. I love to collaborate, and enjoy helping others.
